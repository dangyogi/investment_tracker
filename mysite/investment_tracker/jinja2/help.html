To load up the data from external sources:
  1. Load Transaction History from Vanguard.
    1. Download transactions from Vanguard in a .csv format.
      2. Select the following download options on Vanguard:
      1. Step 1: choose the spreadsheet-compatible CSV file.
      2. Step 2: choose the Custom date range
        • From: the day after the transaction_end_date recorded in the Account table.
        • To: yesterday's date (to avoid partial transactions for today).

      This puts an ofxdownload.csv file in your Downloads directory. Pay attention to which number the browser appends to the filename, for example, "ofxdownload (2).csv".

    2. load_transactions/filename/end_date

      3. Enter the "To" date the you gave Vanguard for the download.

  2. load_fund_history

    3. This automatically goes to finance.yahoo.com as needed to get fund dividend and price history.

  3. update_shares

    4. This creates the AccountShare rows from the data above.
